PACHE reserves the right to vary or amend any of these policies and standards from time to time at its discretion without this nullifying the Contracts of Employment.
Should any of these policies and standards be contrary to the provisions of any statue or law from time to time in force in Nigeria, then the relevant items will have no force or effect. In this event, the rest of the policies and standards will remain valid in all respects
In signing your Contract of Employment when you join PACHE you are in effect confirming that you have read and agreed to the prevailing policies and standards set out in the HANDBOOK and to the Code of Conduct referred to below.
This document applies only to those employed by PACHE on an indefinite or fixed term employment contracts. It does not apply to contractors, consultants, or temporary staff or volunteers.

The policies outlined in this handbook complement those on the HR pages of the corporate intranet (Essential HR). The links to the corporate policies may change from time to time. When this happens, the referenced policy can be found on the corporate intranet by searching for the policy by name.If there are contradictions between this Handbook and Essential HR. the Handbook takes precedence

VISION

To have a brighter and peaceful future for humanity in this crowded, dangerous and beautiful world.

MISSION

To promote and build a world of lasting peace and foster a culture of empowerment, inclusivity, dialogue and service to humanity.
